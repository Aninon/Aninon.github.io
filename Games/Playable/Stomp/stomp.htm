I Made this game almost a year ago! But it uses javascript (and some html5 features). I had to change some stuff around to make it run in this context. It was made for a friend to help support a character she had come up with and wanted to make a stuffed plushie for via kickstarter.

Controls:
  1. Click on the top of the screen to feed him when he gets hungry!
  2. He'll eat on his own!
  3. Click on his waste to remove it or it will pile up and be unsightly!
  4. Click and drag on him to move him around at his annoyance!
