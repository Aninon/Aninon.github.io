Aphids

An RTS whereby the player controls a colony of insects who gather resources to fight and survive against the environment. One such resource being aphids which other colonizing insects (ants at least) use as "honeydew cattle" [Ref]. The Player insect type is unknown. The game has few real-world insects. Inspired by Nintendo's Pikmin

Interval

A time travelling adventure game, taking inspiration from top down zelda games. The player follows the story and various dungeons on a pseudo-procedurally generated landscape. Various points in time cause the landscape to change through a complex terrain generation algorithm. Project on standby due to the immense complexity of the system until I learn more about procedural landscape generation

Town

Working title project for a village simulation system with economy, buildable structures and modifable landscape. Also procedurally generated to an extent, but with a greater reliance on player creations and complex NPC AI
